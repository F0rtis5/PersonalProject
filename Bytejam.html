From Nov. 9th to Nov. 15th 2022, I constructed a project along with a team of 6 other students for an event called Bytejam. The theme for Bytejam this year was "Superheroes" and we decided to build a website. Two highlights of our project were an incorporated Marvel Api that allowed users to search for Marvel characters, the api would return data and comics they were featured in, as well as a survey that submitted data to a google spreadsheet. I built the form and with the help of a second year student we got it working so that the survey data was logged. Below are screenshots of our website and the survey is included at the right.
